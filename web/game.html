Don't be Shy !

Goal :
Place Boos and cover the whole grid with mist.
Blocks may require some Boos to be adjacent to them. Look for the numbers.
Watch out ! Boos are shy and cannot face each other.
If you find that a case cannot contain a Boo, you can place a Luigi. Where Luigi goes, Boos stay hidden !

Commands :
Left click to place/remove a Boo
Right click to place/remove a Luigi
